Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Enter the Average Purchase Value in dollars.
  2. Enter the Average Purchase Frequency (number of purchases per year).
  3. Enter the Average Customer Lifespan in years.
  4. Click the Calculate button to determine the Customer Lifetime Value (CLV).

Formula:

Customer Lifetime Value (CLV) = Average Purchase Value * Average Purchase Frequency * Average Customer Lifespan
