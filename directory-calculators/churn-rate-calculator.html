Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Enter the number of customers at the beginning of the period.
  2. Enter the number of customers at the end of the period.
  3. Click the Calculate button to determine the Churn Rate.

Formula:

Churn Rate (%) = ((Customers at Start - Customers at End) / Customers at Start) * 100%
